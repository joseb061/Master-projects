This is the AStar algorithm for the routing problem. 
It has data of a map containing locations points and ways between them.
The program finds the shortest way from 'Basílica de Santa Maria del Mar (Plaça de Santa Maria)' in Barcelona to the 'Giralda (Calle Mateos Gago)' in Sevilla.

It is made for Joseba Hernández Bravo and Jorge Vicente Puig.
